Cloudiness vs Latitude

This scatter plot shows a few interesting points. Cities near the equator tend to have a smaller percentage of clouds. As we move away from the equator, cloudiness tends to increase but at a proportion that seems similar amongst a variety of latitudes. At +50 to +75 latitude, cloudiness is pretty apparent at 80% - 100% levels.
